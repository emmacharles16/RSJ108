How to conduct and interview!

Time: 60 minutes
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

Have you ever needed to conduct an interview to write a story? It takes a bit of preperation, but it's a lot of fun! Here's everything you need to do to conduct an awesome interview and get all the information you need!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Recorder
  • Notebook
  • Pen or pencil
  • A cool person to talk to
  • A way to talk to get in touch with them
  • A willingness to talk on the fly!

  1. Decide on who to interview

    Always interview more people than you need to. Find people who are involved with, impacted by, etc, the thing that you are covering.

  2. Reach out

    It’s best to set up interviews over email or phone call, but at the end of the day, do whatever you can to get in touch. But remember to be professional!

    A notebook with notes written in it.

  3. Prepare

    Prepare a recording device (separate from your phone), a physical means of taking notes just in case (and to make navigating your recording easier), and of course, the questions you’re going to ask!

  4. Have a natural conversation

    The conversation should be based around the questions you’ve prepared, but don’t stick just to them. Follow up on interesting things that they mention, and try your best to get the information you need.

  5. Transcribe

    There are a lot of different options for transcription services online, and many are free. Use whatever one works best for you!
